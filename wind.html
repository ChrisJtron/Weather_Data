Latitude VS Wind Speed in the Northern Hemisphere






On this particular day, we can see a very slight decrease
in wind speed the further we get from the equator.
This is only noticeable because of the Regression Line on the plot.
To the naked eye, there is no discernable difference
in wind speed based on latitude.
